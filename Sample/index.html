How to Play

This game is a simple visual novel, it works much like a choose your own adventure novel. Press Enter to continue the story, and at some point you may reach a decision point, where you will be able to make a choice that will affect how the story continues.

Controls

Forward Button or 'Enter': Advance to the next line in the story.

Back Button or 'Back Arrow': Look back at the text log (currently not functional).

Help Button: Open up this "How to Play" Screen.

'Space Bar': Hide UI, only show the background.
